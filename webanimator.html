DC Furs Badge Pixel Art Animation Creator v2020-0.3

Instructions:
1. Paint on some pixels.
2. Click the Next Frame button to create a new frame, and paint some changes to the new frame.
3. Click the Play button to preview your animation.
4. Name your animation.
5. Click the Download Animation button to save a .json file containing your animation.
6. Connect the badge via USB and store the YourAnimation.json file in the /animations folder on the badge.
7. Reboot the badge by removing the power source and reconnecting it. Cycle through animations until you find yours!

Notes:
* Delay is in milliseconds, between the current frame with the delay setting and the next frame after it.
* You may save this website to your PC for offline use.
